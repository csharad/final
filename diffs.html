Key Differences

Drip Coffee

This is a cup of drip coffee. Resulting when using a Keurig machine or ordering simple hot coffee. It has the standard measurement of 8oz, and 90mg of caffeine per cup.

Espresso

This is a shot of espresso. Resulting when using an espresso machine / Nespresso or when ordering simple espresso. It has the standard measurement of 1oz. It is much stronger than drip coffee and has approximately 65mg of caffeine per shot.

So, What Do I Pick?

When ordering, consider if you want a coffee or an espresso-based drink. This will typically be a latte, as a latte is two shots of espresso and milk. Drip coffee (or its iced alternative iced coffee) is typically lower in caffeine levels than a standard latte (hot or iced). In addition, you will have less milk and a more watery consistency with coffee than with a latte.
