Various Drinks

There are many drinks that can be made with espresso shots. Although some people choose to enjoy them by themselves, many find them to be too strong and thus dilute them with milk, milk foam, or water. Note that each of these drinks have an equal amount of caffeine, as they are made with equal amounts of espresso shots (typically two). However, decaffeinated espresso is an option for those that are sensitive to caffeine.

Latte

A latte is the most popular espresso based drink. It has espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Flat White

A flat white is very similar to a latte. However, it has no milk foam; it is replaced with more steamed milk.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is also similar to a latte, but it has less steamed milk and more milk foam.

Americano

An americano is a latte but with milk instead of water. Essentially, just espresso diluted with water.
